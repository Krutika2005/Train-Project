About AI Train Traffic Controller

The AI Train Traffic Controller project is designed to modernize and optimize train operations across Indian Railways. By leveraging artificial intelligence, this system can monitor train movements in real-time, detect potential conflicts, and ensure efficient allocation of tracks. It improves safety, reduces delays, and enables smarter scheduling for various types of trains including passenger, freight, and high-speed services. This AI-driven solution represents the next step towards a fully automated and intelligent railway network in India.
